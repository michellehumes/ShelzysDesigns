12-Month Timeline

  • Determine your wedding budget
  • Draft initial guest list
  • Choose wedding date and season
  • Start researching and touring venues
  • Select a wedding style or theme
  • Create inspiration boards and color schemes
  • Draft wedding party list
  • Book ceremony and reception venues
  • Research and contact potential vendors
  • Schedule engagement photoshoot (optional)

9-Month Checklist

  • Finalize guest list and addresses
  • Book key vendors (photographer, caterer, florist)
  • Send Save-the-Date cards
  • Purchase wedding attire and accessories
  • Design and order invitations
  • Reserve hotel room blocks for guests
  • Register for gifts
  • Plan engagement party or bridal shower
  • Begin planning honeymoon

Notes

4

6-Month Checklist

  • Finalize menu and cake tasting
  • Order bridesmaid dresses and groomsmen attire
  • Schedule hair and makeup trials
  • Finalize floral designs and décor concepts
  • Book officiant and finalize ceremony details
  • Reserve rentals (tables, chairs, linens)
  • Order invitations and stationery
  • Create wedding website
  • Finalize hotel accommodations

Notes

5

4-Month Checklist

  • Confirm final guest list
  • Mail invitations
  • Plan and book honeymoon
  • Schedule final dress fittings
  • Review and confirm rentals and décor
  • Start planning rehearsal dinner
  • Finalize wedding cake design and flavors
  • Arrange transportation for wedding day
  • Confirm music playlist with DJ/band

Notes

6

3-Month Checklist

  • Send invitations if not already sent
  • Meet with vendors for final details
  • Finalize ceremony readings and program
  • Plan bachelor and bachelorette parties
  • Schedule hair and makeup appointments
  • Order wedding rings
  • Begin drafting seating chart
  • Finalize décor and flower arrangements

Notes

7

2-Month Checklist

  • Confirm RSVPs and dietary preferences
  • Obtain marriage license
  • Finalize timeline for wedding day
  • Schedule final dress and suit fittings
  • Finalize transportation details
  • Purchase wedding party gifts
  • Plan welcome bags or favors
  • Finalize honeymoon itinerary

Notes

8

1-Month Checklist

  • Attend final dress fitting
  • Create seating chart and place cards
  • Confirm arrival times with all vendors
  • Print programs and menus
  • Write vows and speeches
  • Pack for honeymoon
  • Confirm hair and makeup schedule
  • Prepare payments and tips for vendors

Notes

9

2-Week Checklist

  • Confirm final headcount with caterer
  • Break in wedding shoes
  • Finalize beauty appointments
  • Create wedding day emergency kit
  • Review final playlist and cues
  • Delegate tasks to wedding party and coordinator
  • Prepare marriage license documents
  • Relax and get plenty of rest

Notes

10

Final Week Checklist

  • Pick up wedding attire
  • Pack honeymoon luggage
  • Prepare tip envelopes for vendors
  • Organize décor and supplies for transport
  • Confirm details with vendors and wedding party
  • Do a final walkthrough of venue
  • Ensure rings and marriage license are secure
  • Relax and enjoy the days leading up to the wedding

Notes

11

Day-Of Wedding Checklist

  • Eat a healthy breakfast
  • Allow extra time for hair and makeup
  • Ensure bouquets and boutonnieres are delivered
  • Confirm vendor arrivals and setup
  • Sign marriage license
  • Stay hydrated and enjoy the day!
  • Smile and soak in every moment

  5. Flower Girl and Ring Bearer

Emergency Kit Checklist

  • Safety pins and sewing kit
  • Pain relievers and medication
  • Blotting sheets and face powder
  • Mints or gum
  • Clear tape and glue
  • Phone chargers
  • Stain remover pen
  • Heel protectors and comfortable shoes
  • Bobby pins and hair ties
  • Tissues
  • Band-aids
  • Deodorant
  • Breath spray
  • Static guard
  • Eye drops
