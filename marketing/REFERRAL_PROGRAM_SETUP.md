# Referral Program Setup Guide

Complete guide to launching a referral program for Shelzy's Designs. Perfect for bridal customers who naturally share with friends!

## Why Referrals Work for Shelzy's

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Bridal customers share with bridal parties | 5-10 potential referrals per customer |
| Gift buyers tell gift recipients | Natural word-of-mouth |
| Visual product = social sharing | Instagram/Pinterest ready |

- 10-15% of customers will refer

## Program Structure

### Option A: Simple Give/Get (Recommended)

```
GIVE $10, GET $10

Your friend gets $10 off their first order
You get $10 off your next order

No minimum purchase required
```

**Why this works:**
- Easy to understand
- Generous enough to motivate
- $10 is meaningful on $30+ products
- Both parties win

### Option B: Tiered Rewards

```
REFER MORE, EARN MORE

1 referral ‚Üí $10 off
3 referrals ‚Üí $25 off
5 referrals ‚Üí $50 off + VIP status
10 referrals ‚Üí FREE bottle of choice!
```

**Why this works:**
- Gamifies referrals
- Encourages multiple referrals
- Perfect for brides with large parties

### Option C: Cash Back

```
EARN REAL CASH

Your friend gets 10% off their first order
You get $5 cash back (PayPal or store credit)

No limit on referrals!
```

**Why this works:**
- Cash is universally motivating
- Unlimited earning potential
- Appeals to influencers/content creators

## Recommended: ReferralCandy Setup

### Why ReferralCandy

- Shopify integration (one-click install)
- Automated reward distribution
- Fraud detection
- Analytics dashboard
- Email templates included
- **Cost:** $59/month + 3.5% commission on referred sales

### Setup Steps

1. **Install ReferralCandy** from Shopify App Store

2. **Configure Program Settings:**
   ```
   Advocate Reward: $10 store credit
   Friend Offer: $10 off first order
   Minimum Order: None
   Cookie Duration: 30 days
   ```

3. **Set Up Emails:**
   - Invite email (sent after purchase)
   - Reminder email (7 days later)
   - Reward notification
   - Friend welcome email

4. **Add Referral Widget:**
   - Order confirmation page
   - Customer account page
   - Post-purchase email

5. **Launch & Promote:**
   - Announce to existing customers
   - Add to website footer
   - Include in packaging inserts

## Tracking & Analytics

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Formula |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Referral Rate | 10-15% | (Customers who refer / Total customers) |
| Conversion Rate | 20-30% | (Referred purchases / Referral clicks) |
| Reward Redemption | 70%+ | (Rewards used / Rewards earned) |
| CAC Savings | 50-80% | (Normal CAC - Referral CAC) / Normal CAC |

### Monthly Review Checklist

- [ ] Check total referrals this month
- [ ] Review conversion rate
- [ ] Identify top advocates
- [ ] Send thank you to top referrers
- [ ] Optimize underperforming touchpoints
- [ ] Test new referral messaging
