# Pinterest Pin Design Specifications

## Pin Dimensions & Requirements

### Standard Pin Size
- **Dimensions:** 1000 x 1500 px (2:3 aspect ratio)
- **File Format:** PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **File Size:** Under 20MB (aim for under 2MB for fast loading)
- **Color Mode:** RGB

### Alternative Sizes
| Format | Dimensions | Use Case |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Standard | 1000 x 1500 px | Most pins |
| Long | 1000 x 2100 px | Infographics, step-by-step |
| Square | 1000 x 1000 px | Simple product shots |

**Note:** Pinterest recommends 2:3 ratio. Longer pins may be truncated in feeds.

---

## Design Tool Setup (Canva)

### Creating Your Template Set
1. Go to [Canva](https://canva.com)
2. Create account (free or Pro)
3. Click "Create Design" > "Custom Size" > 1000 x 1500 px
4. Create 10 base templates (instructions below)
5. Save to a folder: "Shelzy's Designs Pin Templates"

### Brand Kit Setup (Canva Pro or Manual)
**Colors:**
- Primary: #2C2C2C (Soft Black)
- Secondary: #F5F1EB (Brushed Beige)
- Accent 1: #E8C4B8 (Blush Apricot)
- Accent 2: #A8B5A0 (Sage Green)
- Accent 3: #B8C5B9 (Dusty Seagrass)
- Accent 4: #C9B8A8 (Sandstone)

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Poppins SemiBold
- Subheadlines: Poppins Medium
- Body: Inter Regular

## Design Best Practices

### Text Guidelines
- **Headline:** 60 characters max (visible in feed)
- **Font size:** Minimum 24pt for readability
- **Contrast:** Ensure text is readable over images
- **Text overlay:** Cover no more than 20% of image

### Image Guidelines
- **Quality:** High-resolution, well-lit photos
- **Source:** Amazon product images, Canva graphics, original photos
- **Consistency:** Use similar editing style across pins
- **Backgrounds:** Clean, uncluttered, on-brand

### Color Usage
- Use brand colors consistently
- Limit to 2-3 colors per pin
- Ensure sufficient contrast
- Match colors to content category:
  - Bridal: Blush + Gold + White
  - Home: Sage + Beige + White
  - Beauty: Soft Pink + White + Black
  - Travel: Ocean Blue + Sand + White

### Logo/Watermark Placement
- Bottom center or bottom right
- Keep small but readable
- Use semi-transparent if over image
- Format: @shelzysdesigns or small logo

## Pin Variations Per Post

For each blog post, create 3-5 different pin designs:

| Pin # | Template | Variation |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | Product Collage | All products, full headline |
| 2 | Product Collage | Different layout, shortened headline |
| 3 | Single Product | Hero product feature |
| 4 | List Style | Top 5 from list |
| 5 | Quote/Review | Best review + product |

### Why Multiple Pins?
- Different designs appeal to different users
- A/B test what works best
- Pinterest favors fresh content
- More pins = more chances to be seen

## File Naming Convention

```
[category]-[post-slug]-[variation]-[date].png
```

**Examples:**
- `bridesmaid-best-proposal-gifts-collage-v1-2024.png`
- `bridesmaid-best-proposal-gifts-single-v2-2024.png`
- `home-palm-beach-decor-list-v1-2024.png`

## Export Settings

### From Canva
1. Click "Share" > "Download"
2. File type: PNG
3. Size: 1x (standard) or 2x (high quality)
4. Transparent background: Off

### From Photoshop/Other
- Format: PNG-24
- Resolution: 72 DPI
- Color profile: sRGB
