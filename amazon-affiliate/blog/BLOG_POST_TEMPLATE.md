## Post Title Formulas

Use these proven title formats for maximum SEO and click-through:

### List Posts
- "10 Best [Product Type] for [Audience] in [Year]"
- "[Number] Must-Have [Products] Every [Person] Needs"
- "Top [Number] Amazon [Products] Under $[Price]"
- "[Number] [Adjective] [Products] That'll [Benefit]"

### How-To/Guide Posts
- "The Ultimate Guide to [Topic/Product Category]"
- "How to Choose the Perfect [Product] for [Occasion]"
- "Everything You Need to Know About [Product Type]"

### Comparison Posts
- "[Product A] vs [Product B]: Which is Best for [Use Case]?"
- "Best [Product Type] Compared: [Year] Buyer's Guide"

### Seasonal/Occasion Posts
- "[Year] [Occasion] Gift Guide: [Number] Ideas She'll Love"
- "Best [Products] for [Season/Holiday] [Year]"
- "[Occasion] Essentials: What You Need and Where to Find It"

## Meta Description Templates

Keep meta descriptions 150-160 characters. Include your primary keyword.

**Template 1 (List):**
"Discover the [number] best [products] for [audience]. From [feature] to [feature], find the perfect [product] for [occasion]. Shop our top picks now!"

**Template 2 (Guide):**
"Looking for the perfect [product]? Our [year] guide covers everything from [topic] to [topic]. Find your ideal [product] today."

**Template 3 (Comparison):**
"Compare the top [products] of [year]. We break down [feature], [feature], and [feature] to help you choose the best [product]."

## Image Requirements

### Hero Image
- Dimensions: 1200 x 628 px (blog header)
- Alt text: Include primary keyword
- File name: primary-keyword-hero.jpg

### Product Images
- Dimensions: 800 x 800 px minimum
- Source: Amazon product images (with attribution) or Canva collages
- Alt text: "[Product name] - [brief description]"

### Pinterest Image
- Dimensions: 1000 x 1500 px (2:3 ratio)
- Include text overlay with post title
- Brand colors and fonts

## SEO Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Primary keyword in title (first 60 characters)
- [ ] Primary keyword in URL slug
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2s and body
- [ ] Meta description written (150-160 chars)
- [ ] All images have alt text with keywords
- [ ] Internal links to 2-3 related posts
- [ ] External link to 1-2 authority sources
- [ ] All affiliate links working and tagged
- [ ] Mobile preview checked
- [ ] Pinterest image uploaded to post

## Publishing Workflow

1. **Draft** - Write using this template
2. **SEO Review** - Complete checklist above
3. **Image Prep** - Create/gather all images
4. **Link Check** - Test all affiliate links
5. **Schedule** - Set publish date
6. **Promote** - Create Pinterest pins
7. **Track** - Note in Master Spreadsheet

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Do:
- Write like a helpful friend sharing favorites
- Use "you" and "your" frequently
- Be specific about benefits and use cases
- Include personal opinions ("We love..." "Our favorite...")
- Create urgency where appropriate ("Limited stock!")

### Don't:
- Use overly salesy language
- Make exaggerated claims
- Write generic descriptions (copy from Amazon thoughtfully)
- Forget to mention potential downsides
- Ignore reader questions in comments
