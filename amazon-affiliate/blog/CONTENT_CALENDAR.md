## Publishing Best Practices

### Optimal Posting Times
- **Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Time:** 9-11 AM in your target audience's timezone
- **Avoid:** Weekends for new content (lower traffic)

### Post Frequency Guidelines
- **Launch Phase:** 2-3 posts per week
- **Growth Phase:** 1-2 posts per week
- **Maintenance Phase:** 1 post per week + updates

### Quality Over Quantity
Better to publish 1 excellent post than 3 mediocre ones. Each post should:
- Thoroughly cover the topic
- Include 8-10 products minimum
- Have optimized images
- Link internally to related content
- Be promoted with Pinterest pins
