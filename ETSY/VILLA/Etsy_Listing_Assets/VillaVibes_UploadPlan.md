## ACCESS PDF APPROACH (Slot 1 — Primary File)

The main `Villa-Vibes-Bundle-Access.pdf` is the critical file. It should contain:

### Page 1: Welcome & Access Link
- Welcome header with bundle name
- Clear, prominent Canva access link (clickable)
- "Click the link above to open all 23 templates in Canva"
- QR code to the Canva link (backup access method)

### Page 2: What's Included Checklist
- Full numbered list of all 23 templates
- Organized by category (Games, Signs, Planning, Extras)
- Checkbox format so buyers can track what they've used

### Page 3: Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Click the Canva link
2. Log in to your free Canva account
3. Click "Use Template" or "Make a Copy"
4. Edit text, colors, fonts, and images
5. Download as PDF (for printing) or PNG/JPG (for sharing)
6. Print at home or at a print shop

### Page 4: Printing Tips & Specs
- Paper size: US Letter (8.5 x 11 in)
- Recommended paper: Cardstock for signs, standard for games
- Printer settings: "Actual Size" (not "Fit to Page")
- Color: Full color recommended

### Page 5: Support & Contact
- "Need help? Message us on Etsy!"
- FAQ answers (access issues, Canva account, printing)
- Link to Shelzy's Designs Etsy shop
- Review request
