## Executive Summary

ToolShed Tested is a WordPress-based power tool review affiliate site launched in 2025. The site shows **strong foundational structure** with good SEO basics, clean design, and proper affiliate disclosure. However, there are **critical revenue optimization opportunities** being missed and several areas requiring immediate attention.

### Quick Stats
- **CMS:** WordPress 6.9
- **Theme:** Custom "toolshed-tested" theme
- **SEO Plugin:** Rank Math
- **Caching:** LiteSpeed Cache 7.6.2
- **Hosting:** Hostinger
- **Total Reviews:** 29+ (as stated)
- **Categories:** 6 (Drills, Saws, Grinders, Sanders, Outdoor Power, Multi-Tools)

### Overall Scores (1-10)

| Category | Score | Notes |
