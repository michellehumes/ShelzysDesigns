How We Make Every Bottle

  1. You place your order with your custom details
  2. We design your personalization layout for perfect balance and spacing
  3. Your design is printed onto special sublimation paper
  4. The bottle is heated, and the ink is infused into the coating (this is sublimation!)
  5. We inspect every bottle for quality
  6. Your bottle is carefully packaged and shipped

The result? A smooth, permanent finish that's part of the bottle—not stuck onto it.
