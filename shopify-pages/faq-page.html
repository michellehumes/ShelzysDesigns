Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: Is the personalization vinyl?

A: No. All designs are created with true sublimation printing, which is permanent and won't peel or crack. The ink is infused directly into the bottle's coating, so your personalization becomes part of the bottle itself.

Q: Will the design peel or fade?

A: No. Unlike vinyl decals, sublimation printing is permanent. The ink is heat-pressed into the bottle's coating, so it won't peel, crack, or fade over time.

Q: Are the bottles dishwasher safe?

A: We recommend hand washing to keep the finish looking its best for years. However, sublimation is far more durable than vinyl and won't peel in normal use. Many customers do put them in the dishwasher without issues, but hand washing ensures longevity.

Q: How long do the bottles keep drinks cold or hot?

A: Our 20oz double-wall insulated bottles keep drinks cold for up to 24 hours and hot for up to 12 hours.

Q: Are the bottles BPA-free?

A: Yes! All bottles are made from BPA-free stainless steel.

Ordering & Customization

Q: How do I personalize my bottle?

A: Simply add your bottle to cart and fill in the customization fields (name, font style, text color, etc.). Enter the name exactly as you'd like it to appear—we'll take care of the design layout.

Q: Can I see a preview before it's made?

A: We design each bottle to be perfectly balanced and professionally centered. If you have specific layout requests or concerns, please contact us before ordering and we'll work with you.

Q: Can I change my order after placing it?

A: If you need to make changes, contact us immediately at hello@shelzysdesigns.com. Once production has started, we may not be able to make changes.

Q: What if I made a typo in the personalization?

A: Please double-check your spelling and capitalization before submitting your order. We print exactly what you enter. If you notice a mistake after ordering, contact us right away—we'll do our best to help if production hasn't started yet.

Returns & Exchanges

A: Because each bottle is custom-made to order, we do not accept returns or exchanges unless there is a defect or error on our part. If there's an issue with your order, please contact us within 7 days of delivery and we'll make it right.

Q: What if my bottle arrives damaged?

A: We pack every order with care, but if your bottle arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement right away.

Q: What if there's a mistake in the personalization?
