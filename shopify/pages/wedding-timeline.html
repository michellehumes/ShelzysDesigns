Common Wedding Order Questions

What if I need to add more bottles to my order?

Contact us within 24 hours of placing your order and we can usually add items. After production starts, you'll need to place a separate order (but we'll do our best to coordinate shipping!).

Can I ship to multiple addresses?

Yes! For bridesmaid gifts shipping to different locations, place separate orders for each address or contact us to arrange split shipping.

What if a name is spelled wrong?

Our design team reviews every order before production. If we spot a potential issue, we'll reach out. After production, we offer a satisfaction guarantee - we'll make it right!

Do you offer bulk discounts for wedding parties?

Yes! Orders of 6+ bottles qualify for special pricing. See our bulk order page for details or contact us for a custom quote.

Can I see a proof before production?

We don't send proofs by default (to keep turnaround fast), but you can request one! Just contact us after ordering. Note: proof requests add 1-2 business days.
