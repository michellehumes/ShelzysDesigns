Our Story

Shelzy's Designs started with a simple frustration: every personalized water bottle I ordered for my sister's bachelorette party arrived with cheap vinyl decals that started peeling within weeks.

I knew there had to be a better way. After months of research and testing, I discovered sublimation printing—a process where the ink actually infuses into the bottle's coating, creating a permanent, dishwasher-safe design that won't crack, peel, or fade.

Today, we've helped thousands of brides, event planners, and businesses create memorable personalized gifts that last. Every bottle is handcrafted in our California studio with the same attention to detail I'd want for my own family's special moments.
