# Product Description Shipping Snippets

Add this section to the END of each product description in Shopify.

---

## Universal Shipping Block (Add to ALL Products)

```html
<h3>Shipping & Processing</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Processing Time:</strong> 3-5 business days (each bottle is made to order)</li>
<li><strong>Shipping:</strong> 2-5 business days after processing</li>
<li><strong>Free Shipping:</strong> On orders over $75</li>
</ul>

<h3>Why Sublimation?</h3>
<p>Unlike vinyl decals that peel, crack, and fade, our sublimation printing infuses the ink directly into the bottle coating. Your personalization becomes part of the bottle - permanent, smooth, and built to last.</p>
```

## How to Add in Shopify

1. **Shopify Admin > Products**
2. Click on each product
3. Scroll to **Description**
4. Switch to **HTML mode** (click `<>` icon)
5. Paste the relevant snippet at the end
6. Click **Save**
