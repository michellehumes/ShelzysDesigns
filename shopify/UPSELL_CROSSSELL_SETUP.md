## 8. A/B Testing Ideas

Test these variations to optimize conversion:

1. **Button Color:** Green vs. Orange vs. Black
2. **Copy:** "Add to Cart" vs. "Yes, Add This!" vs. "I Want This"
3. **Placement:** Above vs. below product info
4. **Offer Type:** Percentage off vs. dollar amount off
5. **Urgency:** Countdown timer vs. "Limited stock" vs. none
6. **Bundle Display:** Grid vs. comparison table vs. single product
