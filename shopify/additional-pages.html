Care Instructions

Keep your personalized bottle looking beautiful for years with these simple care tips.

Daily Cleaning

1

Rinse After Each Use

Empty your bottle and give it a quick rinse with warm water after each use to prevent odors and staining.

2

Hand Wash Recommended

Use warm water, a drop of mild dish soap, and a bottle brush. Gently scrub inside and rinse thoroughly.

3

Air Dry Completely

Leave the cap off and allow your bottle to air dry completely before storing to prevent moisture buildup.

What About the Dishwasher?

💧

Good news: Sublimation printing is far more durable than vinyl decals. Your personalization won't peel off in the dishwasher like vinyl would.

Our recommendation: Hand washing extends the life of your bottle and keeps colors vibrant longer. If you do use a dishwasher, place on the top rack only.

The exterior powder coating and sublimation can withstand occasional dishwasher use, but hand washing is always gentler and preferred.

Deep Cleaning Tips

For Odors

Fill bottle with warm water and 1 tablespoon of baking soda. Let sit overnight, then rinse thoroughly.

For Stains

Mix warm water with white vinegar (1:1 ratio). Let soak for 15-30 minutes, then scrub with a bottle brush.

For Lid & Seal

Remove any silicone gaskets and wash separately. Use a small brush to clean threads and crevices.

For Stubborn Buildup

Drop in a denture cleaning tablet with warm water. Let fizz for 15 minutes, then rinse well.

What to Avoid

❌

Bleach or Chlorine

Harsh chemicals can damage the stainless steel and affect the powder coating.

❌

Abrasive Scrubbers

Steel wool or harsh scrub pads can scratch the exterior finish.

❌

Freezer Storage

Don't put your bottle in the freezer when full - liquids expand and can damage the insulation.

❌

Microwave

Never microwave stainless steel bottles - it's unsafe and will damage your bottle.

❌

Carbonated Beverages

Pressure from fizzy drinks can cause the lid to pop off. Stick to still beverages.

Storage Tips

  • Store with the lid off to allow air circulation
  • Keep in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Don't store with liquids inside for extended periods
  • If storing long-term, ensure completely dry inside
