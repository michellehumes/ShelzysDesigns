# Shelzy's Designs Photography Shot List

**Complete shot list for product photography. Use this for photoshoots or as reference for AI-generated images.**

---

## PHOTOGRAPHY GUIDELINES

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Clean, bright, editorial style**
- **Soft, natural lighting** (avoid harsh shadows)
- **Consistent color palette** (whites, beiges, sage greens, blush)
- **Minimal, intentional props**
- **Focus on the product** (bottles are the star)

### Background Standards
- **Primary:** Clean white or brushed beige (#F7F4EF)
- **Secondary:** Natural textures (marble, light wood, linen)
- **Avoid:** Busy patterns, bright neons, cluttered scenes

### Color Consistency
- All images should feel cohesive
- Use brand colors as accents (sage, blush)
- Keep props neutral or on-brand

---

## CORE PRODUCT SHOTS (Required for Every Bottle)

### Shot 1: Front-Facing Clean Product
**Setup:**
- Bottle centered, straight-on view
- Clean white or beige background
- No props, just the bottle
- Clear view of personalization

**Purpose:** Primary product image for thumbnails and listings

**Notes:** This is the "catalog shot"—clean, simple, professional

---

### Shot 2: ¾ Angle Product Shot
**Setup:**
- Bottle at 45-degree angle
- Shows dimension and depth
- Clean background
- Lighting creates subtle shadow for depth

**Purpose:** Secondary product image showing bottle shape and quality

---

### Shot 3: Close-Up of Personalization
**Setup:**
- Extreme close-up of the name/design
- Shows smooth sublimation finish (no raised edges)
- Focus on detail and quality

**Purpose:** Proves quality—shows no vinyl, no texture, smooth finish

**Critical Detail:** This shot sells the sublimation difference

---

### Shot 4: Lid/Top Detail Shot
**Setup:**
- Top-down or angled shot of the lid
- Shows quality of cap, threading, seal

**Purpose:** Communicates premium quality

---

### Shot 5: In-Hand Scale Shot
**Setup:**
- Female hand holding bottle
- Shows size and scale
- Natural, relaxed pose
- Clean manicure, neutral nail color

**Purpose:** Helps customers visualize size and how it looks in use

**Model Notes:**
- Simple jewelry (if any)
- Neutral clothing (white, beige, sage)
- Focus on the bottle, not the model

---

### Shot 6: Lifestyle/Context Shot
**Setup:** (Varies by product—see specific scenes below)

**Purpose:** Shows bottle in real-life context

**Examples:**
- Bridesmaid bottle: Bridal suite, getting-ready scene
- Kids bottle: Backpack, lunchbox, playground
- Everyday bottle: Desk, gym bag, office
- Holiday bottle: Cozy holiday scene with decor

---

## PRODUCT-SPECIFIC SHOT REQUIREMENTS

### 1. Signature Personalized Bottle

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Front-facing clean product (white background)
2. ¾ angle shot
3. Close-up of name (sublimation detail)
4. Lid detail
5. In-hand scale shot
6. Lifestyle: Desk scene (planner, laptop, coffee)

**Additional Lifestyle Options:**
- Gym bag with towel and earbuds
- Car cupholder
- Outdoor picnic or park bench

---

### 2. Bridesmaid Proposal Bottle

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Front-facing clean product
2. ¾ angle shot
3. Close-up of "Bridesmaid" text
4. Lid detail
5. In-hand scale shot (bridal aesthetic—engagement ring visible)
6. Lifestyle: Bridal suite getting-ready scene

**Bridal Suite Scene Details:**
- White robe or satin robe in background
- Champagne flute or mimosa
- Soft florals (white roses or peonies)
- Natural light from window
- Marble or light wood surface

---

### 3. Bridesmaid Proposal Gift Box

**Minimum 8 Shots:**
1. Closed white gift box (top view)
2. Box slightly open (teaser shot)
3. Fully open box showing all contents (hero shot)
4. Flat lay of all items arranged beautifully
5. Close-up of bottle in box with crinkle paper
6. Close-up of "Will you be my bridesmaid?" card
7. Bottle removed from box, standing next to open box
8. Lifestyle: Hands opening the box (unboxing moment)

**Gift Box Styling:**
- White magnetic gift box
- Blush or white crinkle paper
- Satin scrunchie (blush or sage)
- Mini candle (white or neutral)
- "Will you be my bridesmaid?" card (premium cardstock)

**Flat Lay Composition:**
- All items arranged on white or beige surface
- Symmetrical or intentionally asymmetrical
- Small florals as accent (optional)
- Soft shadows for depth

---

### 4. Bridal Party Bottle Set (5, 8, or 10-Pack)

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Full lineup of all bottles (front-facing, evenly spaced)
2. Grouped cluster of bottles (casual arrangement)
3. Close-up of 2-3 bottles showing different names
4. Single bottle detail (represents the set)
5. Flat lay with bottles + bridal accessories (veils, rings, flowers)
6. Lifestyle: Bridal party holding bottles (outdoor or bridal suite)

**Lineup Shot Details:**
- All bottles same color OR coordinated colors (white, sage, blush)
- Names visible: "Bride," "Bridesmaid," "Maid of Honor," etc.
- Evenly spaced, clean white background
- Soft shadows for dimension

**Lifestyle Group Shot:**
- 4-6 women in white/neutral attire
- Each holding their personalized bottle
- Natural, joyful poses (not stiff)
- Outdoor setting (garden, patio) or bright bridal suite

---

### 5. Everyday Custom Bottle

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Front-facing clean product
2. ¾ angle shot
3. Close-up of personalization (showing icon option)
4. Lid detail
5. In-hand scale shot (casual, everyday setting)
6. Lifestyle: Office desk or gym setting

**Desk Lifestyle Scene:**
- Light wood or white desk
- Bottle next to laptop, notebook, and pen
- Coffee cup or plant as accent
- Soft natural light

**Gym Lifestyle Scene:**
- Bottle on yoga mat or gym bench
- Towel, earbuds, phone as props
- Bright, clean, energetic vibe

---

### 6. Kids Personalized Bottle

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Front-facing clean product (showing kid-friendly icon)
2. ¾ angle shot
3. Close-up of name + icon
4. Lid detail
5. In-hand scale shot (kid's hand holding bottle)
6. Lifestyle: Backpack or lunchbox scene

**Kids Lifestyle Options:**

**Backpack Scene:**
- Colorful backpack (not too busy)
- Bottle in side pocket or next to backpack
- School supplies as props (crayons, lunchbox)
- Bright, playful, clean aesthetic

**Playground Scene:**
- Bottle on park bench or picnic table
- Outdoor natural light
- Playful but not chaotic

**Lunchbox Scene:**
- Bottle next to lunchbox
- Healthy snacks visible (fruit, crackers)
- Bright, clean, organized

### 7. Holiday Sublimation Bottle

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Front-facing clean product (showing holiday design)
2. ¾ angle shot
3. Close-up of holiday design + name
4. Lid detail
5. In-hand scale shot (cozy sweater, holiday vibe)

### 8. Bulk & Corporate Bottles

**Minimum 6 Shots:**
1. Front-facing clean product (showing logo + name)
2. ¾ angle shot
3. Close-up of logo detail
4. Close-up of individual name personalization
5. Group shot of multiple bottles (showing variety of names)
6. Lifestyle: Office or corporate event setting

**Corporate Lifestyle Options:**

**Office Desk:**

## SPECIALTY SCENES (HOMEPAGE & MARKETING)

### Hero Image (Homepage)
**Setup:**
- Single hero bottle (white or sage) with elegant script name
- Clean beige or white background
- Dramatic lighting with soft shadow
- Bottle slightly off-center (rule of thirds)
- Plenty of negative space for text overlay

**Purpose:** Homepage hero—needs space for headline and CTA

---

### Bridesmaid Proposal Scene (Feature Section)
**Setup:**
- Open proposal gift box as focal point
- Bottle, card, scrunchie, candle arranged beautifully
- Soft florals (white or blush roses)
- Bridal-feeling props (ring, invitation, veil)
- Natural light, soft and romantic

**Purpose:** Showcase the proposal box as an experience

---

### Bridal Party Lineup (Feature Section)
**Setup:**
- 6-8 bottles in a row
- Each with different name: Bride, Bridesmaid, Maid of Honor, etc.
- Clean white background
- Evenly spaced, professional product shot

**Purpose:** Shows variety and scale for bridal party orders

---

### Flat Lay (Instagram/Social)
**Setup:**
- Top-down shot
- Bottle as focal point
- Surrounding props: flowers, jewelry, planner, coffee
- Neutral surface (marble, white linen, light wood)
- Symmetrical or intentionally asymmetrical composition

**Purpose:** Social media content, lifestyle branding

---

### Unboxing Moment (Video/Photo)
**Setup:**
- Hands opening white gift box
- Crinkle paper being moved aside
- Bottle being lifted out of box
- Close-up of reaction (optional)

**Purpose:** Captures emotional moment, perfect for video or carousel

## STYLING PROPS GUIDE

### Bridal/Wedding Shoots
✅ **Use:**
- White or blush satin ribbons
- Fresh florals (roses, peonies, eucalyptus)
- Champagne flutes
- White robes
- Elegant stationery
- Engagement rings
- Marble surfaces

❌ **Avoid:**
- Overly trendy props
- Cheap plastic items
- Busy patterns

---

### Everyday/Office Shoots
✅ **Use:**
- Neutral notebooks/planners
- Laptops (modern, clean)
- Coffee cups (white or neutral)
- Simple pens
- Light wood or white desks
- Green plants (monstera, pothos)

❌ **Avoid:**
- Cluttered desks
- Branded items (unless intentional)
- Overly personal items

---

### Kids Shoots
✅ **Use:**
- Colorful but not chaotic backpacks
- Lunchboxes (clean, modern)
- Crayons, colored pencils
- Fresh fruit (as snack props)
- Simple toys (not branded)

❌ **Avoid:**
- Licensed character items
- Overly busy or messy scenes
- Neon colors

---

### Holiday Shoots
✅ **Use:**
- Fresh greenery (pine, eucalyptus)
- Simple ornaments (white, gold, neutral)
- Plaid blankets
- Candles
- Fairy lights
- Neutral gift wrap

❌ **Avoid:**
- Overly kitschy decor
- Bright red/green unless balanced
- Religious symbols (keep inclusive)

## PHOTOGRAPHY TECHNICAL SPECS

### Image Requirements
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2000px on longest side
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 for product cards (Instagram-friendly)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 for hero/banner images
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (under 500KB after compression)

### Lighting
- Soft, diffused natural light preferred
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Consistent lighting across all shots
- Slight shadow for dimension (not flat)

### Editing
- Clean, bright, editorial style
- Consistent color grading across all images
- No heavy filters
- True-to-life colors (especially bottle colors)
- Slight contrast and sharpness for web clarity
