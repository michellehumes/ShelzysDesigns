## PROMPT FORMULA GUIDE

### Base Style Modifiers (Add to Every Prompt)
```
--style: clean, editorial product photography, soft natural lighting, minimal aesthetic, professional e-commerce, neutral color palette
--lighting: soft diffused natural light, bright and airy, subtle shadows for depth
--background: clean white background OR brushed beige (#F7F4EF) seamless backdrop
--quality: high resolution, sharp focus, commercial product photography
```

### MidJourney Specific Parameters
```
--ar 4:5 (for product cards)
--ar 16:9 (for hero/banner images)
--q 2 (quality)
--s 250 (stylization - moderate)
--v 6 (version 6 for photorealism)
```

## CUSTOMIZATION & TIPS

### Changing Names
Replace "Sarah", "Emma", "Shelzy" etc. with your desired name.

### Changing Bottle Colors
- **White bottle:** (default, no change needed)
- **Sage green bottle:** Add "sage green (#9CAE8C)" in place of "white"
- **Blush bottle:** Add "blush pink (#F8D9C5)" in place of "white"
- **Matte black bottle:** Add "matte black" in place of "white"

### Changing Font Styles
- **Elegant script:** (default - flowing, feminine)
- **Bold block:** Replace "elegant script" with "bold clean sans-serif uppercase"
- **Vertical name:** Add "with name running vertically down the length of the bottle"

### Adding Icons
Add to any prompt:
```
with a small heart icon above the name
with a small star icon beside the name
with a small coffee cup icon below the name
with a small palm tree icon next to the name
```

### Adjusting Lighting
- **Brighter:** Add "bright and airy, high-key lighting"
- **Moodier:** Add "soft dramatic lighting, subtle shadows"
- **Golden hour:** Add "warm golden hour lighting, soft sunset glow"

## AI TOOL SPECIFIC NOTES

### For MidJourney:
- Always include `--ar` aspect ratio
- Use `--v 6` for photorealism
- Use `--q 2` for highest quality
- Use `--s 250` for moderate stylization (not too artistic)

### For DALL-E 3:
- Remove MidJourney-specific parameters (`--ar`, `--v`, etc.)
- Keep descriptive language detailed and specific
- DALL-E 3 is better at text rendering (names on bottles)

### For Leonardo AI / Stable Diffusion:
- Use "photorealistic" and "8K resolution" in prompts
- Specify "professional product photography" clearly
- May need to adjust "seed" for consistency
